Welcome to Mass TRAC!

Mass TRAC is an application that provides users of the MBTA with a quick way to see how long they'll have to wait for a train.

Before selecting a station, choose the interface that's right for you:

  • Desktop / Laptop
  • Smartphone / Tablet

Note: the data provided to Mass TRAC from the MBTA is experimental, so predictions can sometimes be wrong. The Mass TRAC authors, nor MassDOT / the MBTA are responsible for inaccurate data.
